Haunted Memories

I have changed my appearance,
changed my style,
changed my body,
changed my smile,
Always in a disguise,
trying to tell you somehow,
your daughter isn't who she says,
you should help her out.
She keeps thinking of the memories,
thinking of what you said,
the things you might've forgotten,
But she never forgave.
I kept it to myself,
Just the way you said,
so they will never get to ask,
Do you think her mother's the same way?
So I bury it down, and deny it to myself,
for someday I'll wake up,
and find out this was all just fake,
But this is so real,
I am not paralysed anymore,
I see it clear as daylight,
They all are coming back to me,
The scream and sounds,
for the person in my room,
sitting in the corner,
hand at her chest,
She just tried to breath.
I was just 13, I never took a breath for myself since,
I was just a kid, why didn't you ask me yourself?
For all the why's and how's you should've asked me somehow,
I wish to have those eid, those birthday's like before,
when i felt complete, with the people i loved the most.
